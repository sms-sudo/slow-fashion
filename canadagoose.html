Canada Goose

Canada Goose

After years of being plagued with issues regarding unethical environmental exploitation and animal abuse, Canada Goose is going through one of its largest image rebranding. From 2022, it will no longer be using its trademarked coyote fur-trimmed hoods made from first-sourced coyotes but instead will be using reclaimed fur already in the supply chain and marketplace. However, only time will tell if these promises will be delivered in 2022.
