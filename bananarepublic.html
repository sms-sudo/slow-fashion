Banana Republic

Banana Republic

A child company to the likes of GAP, Old Navy and Athleta, Banana Republic has notably committed the worst atrocities in the fast fashion market, most notably being a main funder for the “Rana Plaza,” which was an eight story garment factory that collapsed and caused the death of more than 1000 people in 2013. In addition, the sustainably sourced products provided by Banana Republic makeup mere market percents of the overall textile sourcing, showing Banana Republic has no interest in evolving into a sustainable fashion source.
