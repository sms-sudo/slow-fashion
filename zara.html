Zara

Zara

Zara has promised that by 2025, it will only use cotton, linen and polyester that's organic, sustainable or recycled, which is 90% of the raw materials its uses. As of now, Zara is a lead fashion brand that has solidified the rapid pace of that trend-driven business model, known as "fast fashion," which has proved to come at high environmental and social costs.
