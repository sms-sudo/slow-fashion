Rise of Fast Fashion

Fast fashion describes low-cost designs that are quickly transferred from the catwalk to clothing stores. Years ago, there were four fashion ‘trend seasons’ per year, to coincide with the actual seasons. But nowadays, different trends are introduced much more often – sometimes two or three times per month.
